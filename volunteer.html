About the Team

TEDxYouth@Toronto believes that positive change in the world comes from empowered youth. To inspire youth, our volunteers are expected to lead by example: with a demonstrated commitment to the organization, a get-it-done attitude, and a collaborative mindset. Working on the TEDxYouth@Toronto team is lots of fun, but it is a serious commitment as well. We anticipate that the time commitment will be 5 hours per week (varying throughout the year) from March 2016 until November 2017. If you are unable to commit for the full duration but are still interested, please send us an email and we can chat. We are always receive several applications from from high school students interested in helping out with our event.
