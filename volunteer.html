About Our Awesome Team

TEDxYouth@Toronto is the first and longest running TEDxYouth event in Canada. Thanks to our passionate, dedicated, and enthusiastic volunteer team, we have been able to run 7 successful TEDx events in the past 7 years for high school students in the Greater Toronto Area. TEDxYouth@Toronto believes that positive change in the world comes from empowered youth. To inspire youth, our volunteers are expected to lead by example: with a demonstrated commitment to the organization, a get-it-done attitude, and a collaborative mindset. If this is YOU, we would love for you to apply to join our team!

Working on the TEDxYouth@Toronto team is a lot of fun, but it is also a serious commitment. We anticipate that the time commitment will be 5 hours per week (varying throughout the year) from April 2017 until March 2018. As well, we have monthly general meetings for all our team members, and weekly or biweekly meetings for smaller teams. Please consider your personal and professional commitments carefully before you click to apply. If you are unable to commit for the full duration or some of our general meetings but are still interested, please send us an email and we can chat.

We always receive several applications from high school students interested in helping out with our event. However, we are only targeting applicants who have graduated from high school to join our team. If you’re in high school and would like to help out, check back in June for our Youth Ambassador Program applications.

If you have any questions at all about the application process, please get in touch with us at info@tedxyouthtoronto.ca.

The Delegate Experience Coordinator Opportunity

TEDxYouth@Toronto wants to elevate our conference delegates’ experience and we want your help! We are looking for a Delegate Experience Coordinator to understand and analyse the needs of our students and to deliver an enhanced experience for our future conference delegates. You will help the TEDxYouth@Toronto team define metrics that will prioritise our attendees’ experience. You will also spearhead the TEDxYouth@Toronto summer event planning and coordinate the logistics for both the summer and main events. Your successful efforts will ensure that each of our delegates have the best TEDx experience possible.

About the gig

  • Manage Delegate Experience
    • lead the effort to define metrics that identify the success and effectiveness of TEDxYouth@Toronto
    • create event surveys to measure key metrics, analyze feedback from student attendees, and share findings with the greater TEDxYouth@Toronto team
    • develop and implement the plan for following up with delegates after the TEDxYouth@Toronto summer and main events
    • leverage the delegate network as a source of ideas and content to enhance the TEDxYouth@Toronto experience
  • Plan and Execute Summer Event
    • oversee the planning and execution of our third annual TEDxYouth@Toronto summer event (student speaker & delegate recruitment, and event logistics)
  • Prepare Main November Event
    • manage the delegate application process
    • coordinate and plan the logistics and student activities for the November TEDxYouth@Toronto event

You’re Perfect Because You…

  • have a strong history with event planning and logistical coordination
  • have excellent interpersonal and organizational skills
  • find analysis to be second nature and know how to measure and quantify processes
  • are passionate about community development and youth leadership
  • are obsessed with perfecting the experience for our student delegates!
